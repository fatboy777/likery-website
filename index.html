Welcome to Likery

Likery is a prototype app that helps users collect, organize, and discover favorite images from multiple social platforms, including Pinterest.

🧩 What does Likery do?

  • Connects to Pinterest to access users' saved Pins and Boards (with permission)
  • Displays all favorite images in one timeline-like interface
  • Uses AI to recommend similar images based on your collection

🔐 How we use Pinterest data

Likery requests only the minimum permissions necessary to display your saved Pins.
No Pinterest data is stored on our servers. We do not share or sell any user data.
